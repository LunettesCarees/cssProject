FERRORO ROCHER

Our Story

Founded in 1946 in the small town of Alba in the Piedmont region of Italy, Ferrero has a long family tradition of excellence in chocolate and confectionery.

Ferrero's master chocolatiers use their creativity, passion and uncompromising attention to detail to create a wide range of chocolates with distinctive flavors and styles. Their creations are inspired by "Pasticceria", the traditional Italian art of confectionery.

Each Ferrero Rocher is made with the same wish in mind; that each one of them will convey to you all the true feelings of the heart: the thought, the care, the thanks or the unspoken gratitude. May each layer of exquisite taste tell you what is on your mind, and each inch of gold light up every sincere moment.
